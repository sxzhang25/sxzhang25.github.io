about

Hi! I'm a senior at Princeton University studying math and computer science. I'm interested in computer vision, geometric processing and creative technologies.

Currently, I am working in the Princeton Visual AI lab, advised by Professor Olga Russakovsky. Previously, I also worked in the group of Professor Amit Singer, with Amit Moscovich. In the past, I interned on the Android Camera Machine Intelligence team at Google and also spent a great summer at the Williams College SMALL REU.

In my spare time, I make art and short videos. I also enjoy swimming and running (still working on enjoying biking though).
